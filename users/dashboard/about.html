Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Inc.

Unity in Action, Heart in Service.

Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Incorporated (KBKai) is a duly registered, non-profit, non-stock organization recognized by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). Established to serve as a catalyst for positive change, the Association is committed to empowering individuals and families through inclusive, participatory, and sustainable community development initiatives.

The Association seeks to uplift the quality of life of its members by implementing livelihood programs, educational assistance, skills training, and community-based activities that promote self-reliance, resilience, and overall well-being. Through collective action and mutual support, KBKai creates opportunities that enable members to become productive, confident, and socially responsible individuals.

In pursuit of its goals, the Association actively collaborates with private institutions, non-government organizations, and local government units to ensure that programs are responsive to the real needs of the community. Guided by the spirit of bayanihan, Kasangga ng Batang Kankaloo Association Incorporated continues to advocate for inclusive growth, social development, and a better future for all its beneficiaries.

Mission

To uplift the lives of our members by providing accessible livelihood opportunities, educational assistance, skills training, and community development programs, while fostering unity, cooperation, and partnerships with private entities and local government units to promote sustainable growth and holistic well-being.

Vision

A self-reliant, empowered, and compassionate community where every member enjoys an improved quality of life through education, sustainable livelihood, and collective community development.

Core Values

Bayanihan (Unity and Cooperation)

We uphold the spirit of mutual help and collective responsibility, believing that meaningful progress is achieved when members work together toward shared goals.

Integrity and Accountability

We commit to transparency, ethical conduct, and responsible stewardship of resources entrusted to the organization for the benefit of the community.

Empowerment and Self-Reliance

We strive to equip members with knowledge, skills, and opportunities that enable them to become self-sufficient and confident contributors to society.

Inclusivity and Compassion

We respect diversity and ensure that our programs are accessible, fair, and responsive to the needs of all members, especially the marginalized and vulnerable.

Partnership and Collaboration

We value strong partnerships with private institutions, local government units, and other organizations to strengthen our impact and expand opportunities for community development.

Service and Social Responsibility

We are dedicated to serving the community with sincerity and commitment, promoting social development and improving the overall well-being of our members.

Our Story

2025 | The Seed is Planted

In 2025, KBKAI was founded with a clear vision: to uplift children, families, and communities. In the belief that meaningful change starts at the grassroots, KBKAI set out to create programs and partnerships that nurture growth, build resilience, and empower individuals to shape a brighter, more hopeful future.

2025 – Present | Laying the Foundation

Since 2025, our early programs have been dedicated to empowering individuals through education and practical skills training. In partnership with Emmanuel John Institute of Science & Technology, Inc. and JBEST School of Technology and Practical Skills, we focus on building confidence, fostering self-reliance, and equipping our members with knowledge to thrive.

2025 – Present | Caring for Health and Vision

Since 2025, KBKAI has prioritized the health and well-being of its members. In partnership with Esca Optical Shop and LNS Laboratory & Diagnostic Center, we addressed eye care and general health needs, offering members exclusive discounts on services.

2026 – Present | Growing Together

Early this year of 2026, we broadened our impact by launching livelihood programs and community-driven initiatives. Through partnerships with Personal Collection Direct Selling, Inc. and local government units, we strengthened support networks and created opportunities for economic growth.

Our Mission Today

To inspire, support, and empower communities through sustainable initiatives that foster education, livelihood, health, and holistic well-being.
